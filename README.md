## Description
>AccessAble is a web-based application that connects customers with accessability providers such as translators, interpreters, handicap accessable constructions, etc. Customers are able to create and modify their accounts, view listed providers and interact with their pages, and write reviews and rate the provider they worked with. Providers are able to list their services and credentials, read and reply to customer reviews, and view current customers. AccessAble is meant to help customers who need access to accessability features and services, connect with providers who offers the services needed.

## App Functions 
1. Customer
    1. Create/modify customer profile - Register as a customer with an email and password.
    2. View available providers - Browse accesibility services such as interpreting, captioning, and accesibility contractors by ratings and languages for interpeting.
    3. Contact available providers - Provide the phone numbers of these services and forward the customer to the providers contact page.
    4. Write reviews for providers - Review accuracy and efficiency of services, with a 5 star rating system.

2. Provider
    1. Service listing and credentials - Register as a provider and create/modify accounts. List credentials and provide service description. Having tags that the customer can filter for. 
    2. View and reply to reviews/ view ratings - View your customer reviews and reply to them. View the way a customer rates the service. 
    3. View customer retention - View current customers
